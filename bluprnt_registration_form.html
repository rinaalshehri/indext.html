📋 About This Program

BluPrnt is a student-to-student mentorship initiative connecting freshmen and junior students with senior students and recent graduates in the Legal and Technology fields. Our mission is to give every student a personalized career blueprint — starting from their very first year.

This registration form is the first step in your journey. Based on your responses, we will carefully match you with a senior mentor, design your career path, and deliver a set of curated resources tailored to your track and goals.
